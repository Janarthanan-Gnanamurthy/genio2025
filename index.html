About GENIO 2K25

GENIO is Anna University Regional Campus Coimbatore's flagship technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. It brings together students from various institutions to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation.

This year's theme focuses on cutting-edge technologies and innovation in the field of computer science.

Event Schedule

Day 1: Opening ceremony, technical workshops, and coding competitions

Day 2: Paper presentations, project expo, and gaming events

Day 3: Cultural programs, prize distribution, and closing ceremony

Guest Speakers

We have invited industry experts and academic professionals to share their knowledge and experiences with participants.
